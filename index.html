Pickswise | Performance Review & Growth Framework – 2025

1. Context & Objective

In 2025, Google Ads campaigns were launched across multiple regulated US states with a focus on Casino acquisition for Pickswise. Despite strong CTRs and solid lead volumes, overall First Time Depositor (FTD) performance remained inconsistent.

This presentation summarizes the key learnings from 2025 and outlines a revised, data-driven growth strategy focused exclusively on Casino.

3. Key Learnings from 2025

  • High CTRs confirm strong message-market fit in Casino search.
  • Michigan (MI) and Pennsylvania (PA) consistently outperform New Jersey (NJ).
  • Generic Casino keywords outperform bonus-led terms in FTD quality.
  • “Free spins” and “no deposit” keywords drive volume but fail to generate real revenue.
  • FTDs and revenue are highly concentrated in premium brands (FanDuel, BetMGM).

4. Core Problem Identified

The main challenge is not traffic acquisition, but the gap between click → decision → deposit.

Current campaigns successfully attract users but fail to sufficiently:

  • Filter low-intent, bonus-seeking users
  • Support decision-making before brand redirection
  • Optimize toward downstream value instead of surface-level conversions

5. Strategic Shift – New Casino Framework

The proposed strategy reframes Google Ads from a direct conversion engine into a high-intent decision support channel.

Old Funnel

Google Ads → Offers Page → Casino Brand → FTD

New Funnel

Google Ads → Decision-Focused Landing Page → Selected Casino Brand → FTD → LTV

6. Lead Generation – Hybrid Model

Instead of a pure lead generation approach, a hybrid model is proposed to preserve user intent while capturing first-party data.

  • User enters email before seeing casino recommendations
  • User immediately selects a preferred casino brand
  • Instant redirection to the chosen brand
  • Email used for reminder, recovery and premium brand cross-sell

7. New Growth Levers (Google Ads)

  • Landing pages built by intent (slots, fast payouts, high RTP), not only by state
  • Explicit filtering of bonus-only users on landing pages
  • Brand review campaigns targeting late-funnel intent
  • Reduced reliance on free spins and no-deposit queries

8. Measurement & Optimization Framework

Optimization should move beyond Google Ads conversions and focus on real business outcomes.

  • FTD rate per campaign and keyword cluster
  • Revenue per depositor
  • Brand-level LTV contribution
  • Offline conversion imports to Google Ads

9. Q1 Testing Roadmap

  • Focus on MI and PA as primary scaling states
  • Launch intent-based landing pages for generic casino queries
  • Test hybrid lead generation funnel with premium brands only
  • Introduce brand review campaigns for FanDuel and BetMGM
